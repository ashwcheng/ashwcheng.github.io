The zoo is a happy place for everyone, especially for families with kids. The inspiration derived from wanting to better the local entertainment experience that we are so deeply connected with.

THE PROBLEM

We believe that users who arrive at the Santa Barbara Zoo’s website find themselves overwhelmed due to the clashing nature of the layout’s elements and lack of visual guidance.
